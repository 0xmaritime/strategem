TechFlow Solutions - Company Overview

Founded in 2018, TechFlow Solutions is a B2B SaaS company providing workflow automation software for mid-sized manufacturing companies. The company has 150 employees and is headquartered in Austin, Texas.

Product: TechFlow offers a cloud-based platform that integrates with existing ERP systems to automate inventory management, supply chain tracking, and production scheduling. The platform uses AI to predict demand fluctuations and optimize resource allocation.

Market Position: The company serves 250+ manufacturing clients with an average contract value of $85,000 annually. Primary competitors include traditional ERP providers (SAP, Oracle) and newer vertical SaaS companies like FactorySense and ProdOps.

Business Model: TechFlow operates on a subscription model with implementation fees. Gross margins are 72%, with 95% annual retention rate. Customer acquisition is primarily through industry trade shows, LinkedIn outreach, and partnerships with systems integrators.

Growth: Revenue grew 45% YoY to $21M ARR. The company is expanding into adjacent markets including food & beverage processing and is exploring international expansion into Mexico and Canada.

Team: CEO previously led product at a major supply chain software company. CTO has 15 years experience in manufacturing systems. Recent hires include a VP of Sales from a competitor and a Head of Customer Success.

Funding: Raised $12M Series B in 2023, led by Manufacturing Ventures. Total funding to date is $18M. Company is planning to raise Series C in 2025 to fund international expansion.

Key Metrics:
- 250 customers
- $85K ACV
- 95% retention
- 45% YoY growth
- 72% gross margins
- 18 months runway

Challenges: The company is experiencing longer sales cycles (4-6 months vs 2-3 months previously) as economic uncertainty causes manufacturers to delay software purchases. Integration complexity with legacy systems remains a friction point.
